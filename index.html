Andrew Kozak

Interviewed by Emilie Villeneuve

What music do you like to listen to while working?
Study music with some type of beat (kinda pop-ish). I used to listen to Lo-Fi music but it got boring.

Name three designers whose work you find inspiring.

  • Bruno Munari
  • Karel Martens
  • Martin Venezky

Name something (a format, an item) you want to learn how to design but haven't had the chance to try to yet.
Chair, lamp, shelf, clothing, kitchen utensils...etc.

If you were a font, you'd be (you can't say helvetica or comic sans-- both jokes are played). Explain why?
Caslon Regular, perfect font not because I think I'm perfect. It's classy, cool...everything looks good in Caslon.

Name one website that you rely on (try to avoid a platform like google/amazon/pinterest/etc).
Google maps > apple maps. I like seeing how long it'll take me (I usually walk faster).

Have you started making a typeface?
I've made two, one from square brackets [], inspired by a piece of metal. I made another on by squeegeeing onto a window. I brought a caligraphy book to reference and I knew the shop owner because I used to work for them. So when I asked, they said "go for it."
